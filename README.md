# PetTheDog-Unity

## Objective
Pet Bartholomew the dog whilst avoiding the various obstacles and dangers in your way. Most importantly, avoid the watchful gaze of his owner or pay the consequences...

## Features
* Wacky, stretchy arms
* *Home-studio* sound effects
* Hand-drawn art
* A loving dog
